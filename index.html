1. Information We Collect

We aim to keep data collection minimal and transparent:

Location Data (Optional):

The App may request access to your device location to provide accurate prayer times based on your geographical area. This is entirely optional and you can use the app without enabling location services.

Personal Information:

We do not require account registration and do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number within the App.

2. How We Use Information

Any information used by the App is strictly for core functionality:

  • Calculating accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Displaying relevant location information for Islamic practices
  • Maintaining app performance and fixing technical issues
  • Providing personalized Islamic content recommendations

3. Location Permission

Location access is completely optional and under your control:

  • Some features, such as prayer times based on your current location, may not function properly without it
  • You can enable or disable location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • The app will continue to work with manual location input if you prefer

4. Data Sharing and Storage

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes
  • Location data is used only within the App to provide core features
  • No data is transmitted to external servers unless explicitly required for functionality

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use system-level services or libraries (such as device location services) to function properly. These services may handle limited technical data according to their own privacy policies. We ensure any third-party services align with our privacy commitments.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action to remove such information.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices

You have full control over your data and app experience:

  • You can disable location access at any time through device settings
  • You can stop using the App whenever you choose
  • You can contact us to request information about data usage
  • You can request deletion of any data associated with your device

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
